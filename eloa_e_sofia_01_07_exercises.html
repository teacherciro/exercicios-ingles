Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Have you ever had a fight with a close friend? What happened and how did you solve it?
Yes, I had a fight with my best friend last year. We argued about something silly, but we texted each other later and talked about the issues. Now everything is fine between us.
2. Do you think it is important to keep in touch with people you care about? How do you usually do it?
Yes, I think it is very important to keep in touch. I usually text my friends every day and sometimes we meet at the square near my house to spend time together.
3. Describe someone you know who is handsome or funny. Why do you like that person?
My cousin is very handsome and funny. He always makes everyone laugh and he never runs away from problems. He is a great person to be around.
