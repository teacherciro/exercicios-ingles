Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Did you use to have a job where you worked with tools like pliers or screws? Tell me about it.
No, I never used to work with tools. But at that time when I was in school, I used to take a class where we learned how to use a screwdriver and pliers. It was interesting but a little awkward for me because I had never done it before!
2. Have you ever witnessed an injury at work or in public? What happened and what was the main cause?
Yes, I once saw a coworker hurt his hand with a wire at the factory. It was a serious injury and he had to go to the hospital right away. The main cause was that he was not wearing his gloves. It was a very awkward situation for everyone.
3. Do you think employees who gossip at work should be arrested or just punished by the company? Why?
I don't think people should be arrested just for gossiping, but the company should deal with it seriously. Gossip can create a very awkward environment and hurt the main goals of a team. At that time when I worked at my first job, gossip was the main problem in the office.
