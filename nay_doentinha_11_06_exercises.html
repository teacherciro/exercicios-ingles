Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. What clothes did you wash last week? Use 'I washed' in your answer.
I washed my jeans and my T-shirts last week. I also washed my socks and my jacket because there was a big storm and they got wet.
2. Did you hear any strange noise last night? What do you think it was?
Yes, I heard a strange noise last night near the supermarket. I think it was a storm outside. There was a lot of wind and it was very loud.
3. Imagine a robber entered a supermarket. What did he take and what didn't he give back? Use 'didn't' at least once.
The robber took all the clothes and a big machine near the door. He didn't give anything back and he didn't show his face to the cameras.
