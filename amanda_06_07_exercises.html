Yes, I love to spend time outdoors. I usually go for walks in the park or ride my bike on weekends. Sometimes I sit outside and read a book in the sun.
2. What do you know about Norway? Would you like to visit it someday?
Norway is a cold country in northern Europe. It is famous for its beautiful fjords and the northern lights. I would love to visit it someday to see the landscape, but I think the cold weather is a challenge!
3. Imagine you are a surfer. What time do you wake up, and what do you do before you go into the water?
