Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. My told me something interesting about the new project.
A palavra significa 'amigo/colega de trabalho': *COLLEAGUE*
2. We so tired after the meeting that we had to take a break.
Use o passado do verbo 'to be' com o sujeito 'we': *WERE*
3. She something strange coming from the hallway.
Use o passado irregular de 'to hear' (escutar de forma involuntária): *HEARD*
4. I wanted to buy food for my colleagues after the long meeting.
Use a palavra que indica quantidade não específica em frases afirmativas: *SOME*
5. He came to the party and many funny stories about his old job.
Use o passado irregular de 'to tell' (contar): *TOLD*
6. did you find something interesting at the conference?
Use a palavra WH para perguntar sobre 'o quê': *WHAT*
