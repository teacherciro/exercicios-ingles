Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Have you or anyone you know ever been discharged from a hospital? What was the recovery process like?
Yes, my grandmother was discharged from the hospital after a back surgery. Her recovery took about three months. She had to rest, avoid bending forward, and do physical therapy every week to strengthen her backbone.
2. What are some common symptoms of a herniated disc, and what movements — forward or backward bending — should patients avoid?
Common symptoms of a herniated disc include sharp back pain, numbness, and weakness in the legs. Patients should avoid bending forward too quickly, as it puts pressure on the disc and can worsen the pain.
3. Describe a time you sat around a bonfire. What did you eat or drink? Did you add cinnamon to anything?
Last winter, I sat around a bonfire with my family at a farm. We drank hot chocolate with cinnamon on top and roasted corn over the fire. It was very cozy, but I got really sleepy after a while because of the warm fire.
