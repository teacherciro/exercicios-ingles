Perguntas Abertas

1. Did you ever get promoted at work? How did you feel about it?
Yes, I got promoted two years ago. I felt very happy and proud. It was a big responsibility, but I was excited about the new challenges.
2. Do you usually share your feelings with your friends or family? Why or why not?
Yes, I usually share my feelings with my best friend. I think it is very important to talk about how we feel. It helps us feel better and understand ourselves.
3. What do you like to do on vacation? Do you prefer to travel or stay home?
On vacation, I love to travel to the beach. I usually go in the beginning of the year with my family. It is a great time to relax and forget about my responsibilities.
