Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Do you think federations should forbid foreign players from playing as starters? Why or why not?
I think federations should not completely forbid foreign players from playing as starters. Foreign players bring experience and quality to the league. However, I understand that some limits help local players get more opportunities to develop.
2. What does 'to account for the funds' mean? Can you give your own example sentence using it?
It means to explain or show exactly how money was spent. For example: The sports club had to account for all the funds it received from the government partnership last year.
3. What is the difference between 'to report to the government' and 'to file a report with the government'?
Both mean giving information to the government, but 'to file a report' is more formal and specific — it means submitting an official document. 'To report to' is more general and can simply mean informing someone about something.
