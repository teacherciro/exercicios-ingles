Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. The news spread the entire country in just a few hours.
Pense no sentido espacial: 'por todo(a)'. A palavra-chave é *THROUGHOUT*.
2. She stayed calm the job interview.
Pense no sentido temporal: 'durante todo(a)'. A palavra-chave é *THROUGHOUT*.
3. a new heating system in the apartment last week.
Use 'they' como sujeito + o passado regular do verbo *INSTALL*.
4. He his invitation and couldn't enter the party.
Verbo irregular: lose / *LOST* / lost.
5. I selling my old heater — the new one is cheaper but the noise is terrible.
Use o Present Simple do verbo *REGRET* na primeira pessoa (sentimento atual).
6. a lot of noise in the building throughout the night.
Use a estrutura *THERE WAS* para algo no passado + substantivo no singular.

Perguntas Abertas

🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Is there a heater in your home? Where is it, and do you use it throughout the winter?

2. Think of something you regret. What happened, and why do you regret it?
I regret losing touch with an old friend. We were very close, but then we both got busy. I miss her a lot and I want to invite her for coffee soon.
3. Do you root for a sports team? What do you do throughout a game to cheer for them?
Yes, I root for Flamengo! Throughout the game, I cheer loudly and make a lot of noise. When they score a goal, I jump and shout. It is so exciting!
