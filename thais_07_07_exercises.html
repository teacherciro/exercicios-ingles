Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. My grandma listens to music the radio every morning.
Use *ON* para falar de eletrônicos e mídias.
2. We had dinner the restaurant near our house.
Use *AT* para locais com endereço específico.
3. My grandfather a letter to my grandma last week.
Passado irregular do verbo *WRITE*.
4. She travel last summer because she was sick.
Use *DIDN'T* para negação no passado seguido do verbo no presente.
5. Did your grandparents to Brazil last year?
Em perguntas com DID, use o verbo no PRESENTE. O passado de fly é *FLEW*, mas aqui usamos a forma base.
6. I wanted my grandpa, but he was busy.
Entre dois verbos, use *TO* + infinitivo.

Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. What did you do last weekend? Use at least two past tense verbs in your answer.
Last weekend, I stayed home. I watched a movie on TV and ate pizza with my family. I didn't go out because it was raining.
2. Tell me about your grandparents. Where do they live — near or far from you? Did you visit them recently?
My grandparents live near my house. I visited them last Saturday. We talked a lot, ate lunch together, and they told me old stories. I didn't want to leave!
3. Did you travel last year? Where did you go and what did you buy?
Yes, I traveled to Rio de Janeiro last year. I flew there and stayed at a hotel near the beach. I bought some souvenirs and ate a lot of delicious food.
