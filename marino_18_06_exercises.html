Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Have you ever given or received a gift card? Was it a good gift, or do you prefer something more personal? Give a reason for your answer.
Yes, I once received a gift card for a bookstore. I thought it was a great gift because I could choose exactly what I wanted. My reason for liking it was that I hate returning presents that don't fit.
2. Describe a situation where someone was overreacting. What were their complaints, and do you think their reaction was fair?
My friend was overreacting when the coach put him on the bench. He made a big speech about being the best player on the team, but the real reason was just that he was tired from the last game.
3. In your opinion, what is the difference between a funeral and a burial? Can you describe a typical funeral or burial tradition in Brazil?
A funeral is the ceremony where people say goodbye, and a burial is when the body is placed in the ground. In Brazil, both usually happen on the same day. People wear dark clothes, give speeches, and sometimes bring flowers to the burial.
