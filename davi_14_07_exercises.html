Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. The oil pressure gauge was zero. Something was seriously wrong with the engine.
Use o verbo mais idiomático para o que um instrumento de medição indica: *READING*
2. The dashboard was a high temperature warning, so we pulled over immediately.
Use o verbo neutro para o que aparece em um painel ou display: *SHOWING*
3. they replace the transmission or the car is going to break down on the highway.
Inicie a estrutura 'ou...ou' com: *EITHER*
4. The mechanic said he could fix it neither quickly cheaply — it was a complicated job.
Complete a estrutura 'nem...nem' com o segundo conector: *NOR*
5. Back then, there was no AC in the shop, so all the mechanics through every summer without complaining.
Passado simples do verbo 'to sweat' (suar): *SWEATED*
6. I hate dealing with unexpected car problems. do I — they always cost way more than you expect.
Concorde com a frase negativa usando 'nem eu / eu também não': *NEITHER*

Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Think of a car, machine, or device you use. Describe what the gauge or display was reading or showing at some point.
The temperature gauge was reading way too high, and the dashboard was showing a red warning light. We stopped the car right away.
2. Use 'either...or' and 'neither...nor' to talk about a problem or difficult choice you faced.
Either I call the mechanic or I take the bus to work. The mechanic said neither the part nor the labor was cheap, so I chose the bus.
3. What was different about everyday life 'back then' — when your parents or grandparents were young? Tell me two things using the past simple.
Back then, people didn't use smartphones. They sweated through the summer without AC and had no GPS to find their way around.
