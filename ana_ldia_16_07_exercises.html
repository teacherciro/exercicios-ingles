Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. I travel to Barretos in August.
Use o auxiliar de FUTURO — *WILL*
2. The car into the truck on the left side of the road.
O carro BATEU no caminhão — use o passado de 'to crash' — *CRASHED*
3. You drive without a license — it's not worth the risk!
Recomendação negativa: você NÃO DEVERIA dirigir sem carteira — *SHOULDN'T*
4. The gas station is of the highway.
O posto de gasolina fica no LADO ESQUERDO da estrada — *LEFT SIDE*
5. It was my — I crashed the car near the gas station.
Foi CULPA minha — use *FAULT*
6. There was so much behind the gas station that I couldn't sleep.
Havia muito BARULHO atrás do posto — *NOISE*

Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Will you travel somewhere this year? Where will you go?
Yes, I will travel to São Paulo in December. I will visit my uncle and his family there.
2. What should a driver do if their car stops near a gas station?
The driver should stop the car safely on the left side of the road and call for help. They shouldn't panic — it's not worth it.
3. Can you drive a car? Could you drive before? Tell me about it.
Yes, I can drive a car. I could drive when I was eighteen. One time I crashed my car — but it was not my fault. There was too much noise on the road and I lost focus.
