Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. How has learning English affected your self-esteem? Do you feel like you have 'unlocked' a more confident version of yourself?
Honestly, learning English has done a lot for my self-esteem. Long story short, I used to feel really insecure when I had to speak in front of people, but now I feel like I have unlocked a more confident version of myself. My mental health has also improved because I feel more connected to the world.
2. What is the difference between 'to talk,' 'to speak,' 'to tell,' and 'to say'? Try to give one example for each.
Sure! 'Talk' is informal and conversational: I talked to my friend yesterday. 'Speak' is formal, especially for languages or presentations: She speaks three languages. 'Tell' is followed by a person: He told me the news. 'Say' is followed by words or uses 'to someone': She said goodbye to me.
3. Have you ever had to leave a place or situation that was affecting your mental health? What happened, and how did you feel afterward?
Yes, I once had to leave a job because it was really damaging my mental health. Long story short, my boss was not kind at all, and I started to regress emotionally. After I left, I slowly unlocked a calmer and happier version of myself, and my self-esteem improved a lot.
