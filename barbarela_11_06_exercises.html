Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. She always takes care her hair before going to bed.
O verbo 'take care' sempre é seguido da preposição *OF* para indicar de quem ou do que se cuida.
2. Barbarela loves to her nails before hanging out with friends.
Barbarela adora *PINTAR* as unhas antes de sair com os amigos.
3. The new advertising campaign helped the company its profits significantly.
A nova campanha publicitária ajudou a empresa a *AUMENTAR* seus lucros significativamente.
4. It took her a few weeks to get to waking up early regularly.
Demorou algumas semanas para ela se *ACOSTUMAR* a acordar cedo regularmente. A estrutura é: get USED to + verbo -ING.
5. She stayed all night during the music festival.
Ela ficou *ACORDADA* a noite toda durante o festival. 'Stay UP' é o phrasal verb completo.
6. The hair salon charges a small for the conditioner treatment.
O salão cobra uma pequena *TAXA* pelo tratamento com condicionador.

4
Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. How do you take care of your hair? Do you use a conditioner regularly?
I take care of my hair every day. I wash it regularly and always use conditioner. I try not to use the dryer too often because it can obviously cause a lot of damage. Finding the right way to care for your hair takes time, but it is worth everything.
2. Have you ever stayed up all night? What were you doing, and how did you feel the next day?
Yes, I stayed up all night once during an important event. I was hanging out with friends and we lost track of time. The next day I was exhausted and far away from my best self. I got used to sleeping less that week, but it was not easy.
3. Do you think advertising campaigns influence the way women take care of themselves? Why or why not?
Yes, I think advertising campaigns have a huge influence on women. Companies profit a lot by making women feel like they need to buy everything. Obviously, we should be careful not to increase unnecessary spending just because of ads.
