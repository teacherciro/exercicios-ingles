Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Have you ever had to deal with an injury or health issue that changed your exercise routine? What did you do to get back to your normal activities?
Yes, I had a herniated disc and had to do physiotherapy for a few months. It was kinda boring at first because the exercises were so repetitive, but I ended up getting back to the gym after I strengthened my back muscles.
2. When you watch a soccer match, do you prefer the first half or the second half? Why? Have you ever seen a team come back and win after being behind?
I definitely prefer the second half because it's more exciting. Teams usually come back and try harder to win. I once watched a match where the referee stopped the game three times, but the home team still came back and won in the last few minutes.
3. How do you use a planner to organize your week? What kinds of events or activities do you usually include, and do you think having a routine is important?
I use a planner on my phone to organize my physiotherapy sessions, gym workouts, and work meetings. I think having a routine is really important, especially when you are recovering from an injury, because it helps you stay consistent until you reach your goal.
