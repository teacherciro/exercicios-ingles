Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. What is the difference between a 'wage' and a 'salary'? Can you give one example of each?
A wage is hourly pay, like a waiter who earns $15 per hour. A salary is a fixed annual amount, like a teacher who earns $50,000 per year.
2. What are you afraid of? Use 'I'm afraid of' at least twice in your answer.
I'm afraid of spiders because they move very fast. I'm also afraid of flying, but I want to travel more, so I need to practice.
3. Think of something you did recently that took a long time. Describe it using 'It took...'
Last week I cleaned my whole house. It took almost four hours. I was very tired after that, but it took only one hour to cook dinner.
