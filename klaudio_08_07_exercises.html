Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. The stood next to the bride at the altar during the ceremony.
Dica: *GROOM* é o noivo específico da cerimônia de casamento.
2. They decided to a big party to celebrate their engagement.
Dica: *THROW A PARTY* significa 'fazer uma festa'. Use o infinitivo do verbo (forma base).
3. He got down on one knee and decided to to his girlfriend at the restaurant.
Dica: *PROPOSE* significa pedir alguém em casamento. Use o infinitivo e lembre da preposição que vem depois.
4. The bar was so that we couldn't find a seat anywhere.
Dica: *PACKED* descreve um lugar completamente cheio/lotado de pessoas.
5. The coach the players from eating junk food before the game.
Dica: *FORBADE* é o passado simples do verbo irregular 'to forbid' (proibir).
6. She is his — they plan to get married next summer.
Dica: *FIANCEE* significa noiva/noivo antes do casamento (durante o noivado).

Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Do you prefer big weddings or small weddings? Why?
I prefer small weddings because they feel more personal. I don't like places that are too packed with people. A small ceremony with close family and friends is more special to me.
2. Describe a party you threw or attended. Was it packed? What did people drink?
Last year, I threw a birthday party for a friend. The place was completely packed — almost 40 people showed up! We had sparkling wine and cold beer in pints. Everyone had a great time.
3. If you were a referee, what would you forbid players from doing? Why?
If I were a referee, I would forbid players from arguing with my decisions on the field. That kind of behavior bothers everyone at the game and slows everything down.
