Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. It's hot and today. I love Summer!
O dia está ensolarado — use o adjetivo *SUNNY*
2. Amanda and Johnny want to a nice apartment downtown.
Eles querem alugar — use o verbo *RENT*
3. I don't have money to go on vacation this Summer.
Não tenho dinheiro suficiente — use a palavra *ENOUGH*
4. My family lives far from here. I miss them a lot.
Minha família mora longe daqui — use a palavra *AWAY*
5. I go to the beach with my friends this Summer!
Forma informal de 'want to' — use *WANNA*
6. At the of Spring, the weather starts to get warmer.
No fim da primavera — use a palavra *END*

Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. What do you want to do this Summer? Do you want to travel or stay home?
I want to travel this Summer. I wanna go to the beach and hang out with my friends every day. I don't want to stay away from home for too long, though.
2. Which season do you like the most and why? What do you usually do during that season?
I like Summer the most because it's always sunny and warm. I usually hang out with my friends at the pool. I also want to rent a house near the beach someday.
3. What are your plans for the future? Use 'will' or 'going to' in your answer.
I am going to study English every day because I want to be fluent. I will also try to hang out with English speakers to practice. Next year, I'm going to rent a bigger apartment.
