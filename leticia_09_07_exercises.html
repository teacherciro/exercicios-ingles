Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. Yesterday, I 15 kilometers with my husband.
Use o passado do verbo *WALK* (regular: adicione -ed).
2. I the homework, but I translated the text.
Use a negativa do passado: *DIDN'T* + verbo base DO.
3. This week I washed clothes, I did my homework, and I the law.
Use o passado do verbo *STUDY*. Dica: verbos terminados em -y trocam o y por -ied.
4. She her friends this week.
Forma negativa: *DIDN'T* + verbo base MEET (encontrar).
5. Every morning, I at 6 a.m. and then I have breakfast.
O phrasal verb *WAKE UP* significa acordar.
6. I didn't last night because I was tired.
O phrasal verb *STAY UP* significa ficar acordado.

Perguntas Abertas

🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. What did you do last week? Use at least 3 verbs in your answer.
Last week I studied English, I washed my clothes, and I walked in the park. I didn't work on Friday.
2. What time do you usually wake up and get up? Do you have breakfast every day?
I usually wake up at 6 a.m. and I get up at 6:15. Yes, I always have breakfast. I drink coffee and I eat bread.
3. What didn't you do this week that you wanted to do?
This week I didn't run and I didn't call my friend. I wanted to exercise, but I didn't have time.
