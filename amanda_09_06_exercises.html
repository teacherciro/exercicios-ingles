Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. I travel to Europe next summer. (use will)
Use o auxiliar *WILL* para expressar uma intenção futura. Estrutura: sujeito + WILL + verbo.
2. She going to watch The Devil Wears Prada tonight.
Complete com o verbo TO BE correto. Para 'she', usamos *IS*.
3. I travel to Japan next month. (negation of will)
Use a forma negativa e contraída de will not: *WON'T*.
4. Are you buy the apartment?
Complete a pergunta com a estrutura *GOING TO* para expressar um plano futuro.
5. He going to buy the apartment. (negation)
Negue usando IS + NOT contraído: *ISN'T*.
6. I'm travel to Japan. (informal form)
Use a forma INFORMAL e contraída de 'going to': *GONNA*.

4
Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. What are you going to do this weekend? Tell me at least two plans.
This weekend, I am going to visit my family and I am going to watch a movie at home. I won't go out because I want to rest.
2. Will you travel somewhere next year? Where will you go and what will you do there?
Yes, I will travel to Buenos Aires next year. I will visit the city center and I will try traditional Argentine food. I won't stay in a hotel — I am going to rent an apartment.
3. Think about a personal goal you have for the future. Use both 'going to' and 'will' to describe your plans.
I am going to study English every day because I want to be fluent. I will practice speaking with my teacher and I'm gonna watch movies in English without subtitles.
