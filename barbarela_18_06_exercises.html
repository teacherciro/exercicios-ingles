Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. The Mundo Lingo event in many countries around the world.
Use o verbo que significa 'acontecer' para eventos (terceira pessoa do singular): *TAKES PLACE*
2. I am go to the bar tonight. Want to come?
Forma informal e coloquial de 'going to': *GONNA*
3. He hasn't been to the laundromat
Palavra usada no final de frases negativas no Present Perfect para indicar algo que ainda não aconteceu: *YET*
4. I've done harder things than this.
Palavra usada entre have/has e o particípio em frases afirmativas no Present Perfect: *ALREADY*
5. She put a on her water bottle as a decoration.
Um pequeno objeto adesivo decorativo: *STICKER* (adesivo)
6. There a language exchange event downtown next Friday.
Estrutura de futuro com 'there' para indicar que algo vai existir ou acontecer: *WILL BE*

4
Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Have you ever been to a language exchange event like Mundo Lingo? What happened there?
Yes, I have! I went to a Mundo Lingo event downtown last month. It takes place every week and people from many countries are there. I met some interesting colleagues and even made a close friend.
2. What are you gonna do this weekend? Are there any events taking place downtown?
I'm gonna meet some friends downtown this weekend. There will be a street fair near my neighborhood, so we are going to check it out. I've already been there before and it's really fun.
3. Tell me about a gift you received that was special to you. What was it and who gave it to you?
A close friend gave me a sunflower and some stickers last year. I kept the stickers on my notebook because they were really pretty. It wasn't a big gift, but it made me really happy.
