Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. My dad used to the fireplace every single winter evening.
Use o verbo que significa 'acender'. Depois de 'used to', o verbo vem no infinitivo: *LIGHT*.
2. The baker kneaded the for thirty minutes before putting it in the oven.
A palavra significa 'massa' (de pão ou pizza). Lembre-se: *DOUGH* se pronuncia 'dou'.
3. My grandmother to knit a warm blanket for every grandchild.
Complete com o auxiliar correto para expressar hábito no passado. A estrutura é *USED* to + verbo.
4. The loud construction noise next door everyone in the neighborhood all morning long.
Use o passado simples do verbo que significa 'perturbar': *DISTURBED*.
5. He her for almost three years before proposing.
Use o passado do verbo que significa 'namorar': *DATED*.
6. Allow me to you to my colleague Ana from the downtown brewery.
Use o verbo que significa 'apresentar (pessoas)': *INTRODUCE*.

Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Did you use to have any special habits or routines when you were a child? Describe at least two things using 'used to'.
When I was a child, I used to wake up early and watch cartoons every Saturday morning. I also used to help my mom make dough for homemade bread. Those were great memories!
2. How do you usually stay healthy? Did you use to do anything different to take care of your health in the past?
I usually exercise three times a week and try to eat healthy food. I used to eat a lot of junk food when I was a teenager, but I changed my habits. Now I feel much better!
3. Imagine you are at a party and you want to introduce a friend to someone new. What would you say? Try to use 'introduce' and at least two words from today's vocabulary.
Hey Sarah, let me introduce you to my friend Carla! She works at a brewery downtown and she usually makes amazing homemade dough on weekends. You two should definitely talk!
