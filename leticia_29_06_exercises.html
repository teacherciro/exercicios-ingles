Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. There is sugar in this dessert. It is very sweet!
Use *A LOT OF* — funciona com substantivos contáveis e incontáveis. 'Sugar' é incontável.
2. I don't like to wake up . I prefer to sleep until 9am.
A palavra significa *CEDO* em inglês.
3. She like to wake up early, so she goes to bed early.
Negativa no presente com 'she' usa *DOESN'T* + verbo base (sem 's').
4. I a lot last week. I was very tired on Friday.
Passado regular de 'to work': adicione *-ED* ao verbo.
5. I didn't home last night. I went to a restaurant.
Depois de *DIDN'T*, use o verbo na forma base (presente), não no passado.
6. you live in Itajai before moving here?
Pergunta no passado simples começa com *DID*.

Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Do you like to wake up early? What time do you usually wake up?
I don't like to wake up early. I usually wake up at 8am. I think that is a good time for me.
2. What did you do last weekend? Try to use at least one regular verb and one irregular verb.
Last weekend, I walked to the park and then I went to a restaurant for lunch. I also worked a little at home in the afternoon.
3. Do you eat a lot of sugar? Do you drink much water every day?
I don't eat much sugar, but I drink a lot of water every day. I think drinking water is very important for our health.
