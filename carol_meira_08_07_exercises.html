Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. I read about that new Italian restaurant downtown and it delicious! We should go.
Você leu sobre o restaurante — é informação escrita, não visual. Use o verbo *SOUND* no tempo correto (terceira pessoa do presente).
2. The physical therapist said swimming is the best exercise to your backbone muscles.
O verbo significa 'fortalecer'. Use *STRENGTHEN* no infinitivo após 'to'.
3. She didn't sleep at all last night. She exhausted today.
Você está observando vários sinais: olhos cansados, voz baixa, sem energia. Use o verbo *SEEM* no tempo correto.
4. At the dinner party, the welcomed everyone at the door and made sure they all had drinks.
Quem recebe os convidados em casa é o *HOST* (anfitrião).
5. I'm to hear that you passed your English test, Carol!
Complete a expressão fixa: 'I'm *GLAD* to hear that' = Fico contente em ouvir isso.
6. I turned off all my before going to bed because they were keeping me awake.
Palavra no plural. Singular: *NOTIFICATION* = notificação.

Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Think about someone you saw or talked to recently. How do they look, sound, or seem? Use at least two of the three verbs in your answer.
My coworker looks really tired lately — she has dark circles under her eyes. When she talks, she seems distracted, like her mind is somewhere else. But today she called me and she sounded much happier, so I think she's doing better.
2. Have you ever been a host for a dinner, party, or event? What did you do to prepare? If not, describe what you would do.
Yes! Last month I hosted a small birthday dinner for my friend. I had to send notifications to all the guests, cook the food, and decorate the apartment. It was a lot of work, but I'm glad I did it — everyone had a great time!
3. What are some things you do — or would like to do — to strengthen your body or your mind? Use the word 'strengthen' at least once.
I go for walks every morning to strengthen my legs and clear my head. I also try to strengthen my English by practicing every day with my teacher and reviewing my lesson notes at night.
