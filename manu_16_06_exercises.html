Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Tell me about a time when you had to depend on someone else for help. What happened and how did that person help you?
Last year, I had to depend on my sister when I was sick. She took turns with my mom taking care of me for a week and cooked all my meals. I'm really grateful for their help — I healed much faster because of them.
2. If you had a big budget to spend on a trip, where would you go? What would you order at a restaurant there?
If I had a big budget, I would go to Italy. I would order fresh pasta with a homemade tomato sauce at a local contemporary restaurant. I would also hire a private guide to show me around the city.
3. What is your favorite sauce or seasoning to use when cooking? How do you usually season your food at home?
My favorite sauce is a garlic and herb sauce that my mom makes. I usually season my food with salt, pepper, and olive oil. I think good seasoning is the key to a better dish — it can completely change the flavor.
