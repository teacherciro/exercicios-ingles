Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. I finished the report. I went to lunch.
Algo aconteceu DEPOIS DISSO — use o advérbio *AFTERWARDS*
2. The city is beautiful. it's very safe.
Estamos ADICIONANDO mais um ponto positivo sobre a cidade — *FURTHERMORE* (ALÉM DISSO)
3. She missed the exam. she failed the course.
A segunda frase é uma CONSEQUÊNCIA da primeira — *THEREFORE* (PORTANTO)
4. Curitiba is very cold in winter. it rains a lot.
Adicionando MAIS UMA informação sobre o mesmo tema — *MOREOVER* (ALÉM DISSO)
5. I don't want to go to that party. it's too far from home.
Estamos dando MAIS UM MOTIVO para não ir — *BESIDES* (ALÉM DISSO)
6. Don't worry. I can this situation.
O verbo significa LIDAR COM algo — *HANDLE*

Tradução
🌎
🔁 Traduza as frases! Atenção na direção.
🇧🇷 → 🇺🇸   Depois disso, fui dormir.
🇺🇸 → 🇧🇷   She doesn't like her new job. Besides, the salary is very low.
🇧🇷 → 🇺🇸   O apartamento é espaçoso. Além disso, tem uma vista linda.
🇺🇸 → 🇧🇷   Next Saturday I'll travel. Therefore, we won't have class.
🇧🇷 → 🇺🇸   Você sabe lidar com esse tipo de situação?
🇺🇸 → 🇧🇷   The course is challenging. Moreover, it requires a lot of dedication.

Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Think about your routine last week. What did you do after work or after studying? Use 'afterwards' or 'after + noun' in your answer.
After work, I went to the gym. Afterwards, I cooked dinner and watched a series on Netflix before going to bed.
2. Describe a decision you made recently that had a clear consequence. Use 'therefore' to connect the reason and the result.
I was feeling very tired last weekend. Therefore, I cancelled my plans and stayed home to rest.
3. Talk about a city, a place, or a person you admire. Use at least two connectors from today's class (besides, furthermore, or moreover) to add extra information.
I really love São Paulo. The city has amazing restaurants and a great nightlife. Furthermore, it has world-class museums and art galleries. Moreover, there are incredible opportunities for work and business.
