Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. She was for two weeks after the accident.
Ela ficou ___ por duas semanas. Vocabulário da aula que significa 'internada/hospitalizada': *HOSPITALIZED*
2. He his coat before entering the house.
Ele ___ o casaco antes de entrar. Phrasal verb que significa 'tirar peça de roupa' no passado: *TOOK OFF*
3. My nephew to the store and bought some hazelnut pieces.
Meu sobrinho ___ à loja. Use o passado irregular de 'to go': *WENT*
4. She polished her nails and a beautiful dress to the party.
Ela fez as unhas e ___ um vestido bonito. Use o passado de 'to wear' (vestir/usar): *WORE*
5. I prefer not my emails late at night.
Eu prefiro não ___ meus emails tarde da noite. Lembre da regra: NOT + *TO CHECK* (infinitivo)
6. After the long flight, she just wanted to and rest.
Depois do longo voo, ela só queria ___. Phrasal verb que significa 'deitar': *LAY DOWN*

Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. What did you have for breakfast today? Use at least two verbs from the lesson in your answer.
Today I had eggs and toast for breakfast. I also read the news on my phone and sent a message to my sister before leaving home.
2. Describe what you wore yesterday. Did you put on anything special or take anything off when you got home?
Yesterday I wore jeans and a blue blouse. I also put on a light jacket because it was kinda cold outside. When I got home, I took off my jacket and laid down on the couch for a while.
3. Have you ever had trouble sleeping? What do you prefer not to do before bed?
Yes, sometimes I have trouble sleeping. I prefer not to drink coffee at night, and I also try not to check my phone in bed. I usually lay down and read a book — it helps a lot.
