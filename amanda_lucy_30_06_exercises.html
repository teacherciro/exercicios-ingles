Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. It's so warm outside! I go swimming at the beach today.
Use a forma informal e coloquial de 'want to': *WANNA*
2. Have you missed someone so much that it hurt?
Use a palavra que indica 'alguma vez já' em perguntas: *EVER*
3. The weather is warm today, but tomorrow it will be even .
Use o comparativo do adjetivo 'warm' (quente): *WARMER*
4. She loves this city so much. She learned Portuguese just to live here!
Use a palavra que significa 'até mesmo' em frases afirmativas: *EVEN*
5. watch a movie tonight! I'm so excited!
Use a expressão para fazer sugestões e convites: *LET'S*
6. By the way, I'm bring some water and snacks to the picnic.
Use a forma informal de 'going to' para falar de planos: *GONNA*

4
Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Have you ever missed a friend or family member so much that you called them right away? Tell me about it!
Yes! I once missed my best friend so much when she moved to another city. I even called her every single day for a month because I was so excited to talk to her. She is the best friend ever!
2. Do you prefer warm weather or cold weather? What do you wanna do on your favorite kind of day?
I prefer warm weather! On a warm day, I wanna go to the beach, feel the water, and just enjoy everything. I'm even gonna learn how to surf someday. Warm days are the best ever!
3. If you could plan a fun day out with your friends, what would you suggest? Use 'Let's' to describe at least three activities!
Let's walk along the beach in the morning, let's enjoy some ice cream — it's the best ice cream ever, by the way — and let's watch the sunset together at night. The more the merrier, so I'm gonna bring everyone I know!
