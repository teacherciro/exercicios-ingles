Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. This room is a complete . Let's clean it up later.
A sala está uma bagunça! Use a palavra *MESS*.
2. I need to finish report before dinner.
O relatório é MEU. Use o possessivo *MY* e lembre: sem THE antes dele!
3. The kids left toys everywhere in the living room.
Os brinquedos são DELES (das crianças). Use o possessivo *THEIR*.
4. Let's a wine and relax tonight.
Vamos BEBER um vinho. Use *HAVE* antes de bebida — neste contexto significa beber.
5. Maria introduced her at the party. They are getting married next year.
Ela apresentou seu NOIVO na festa. A palavra correta é *FIANCEE*.
6. Excuse me, where is the ? I need to wash my hands.
Você está em um restaurante e precisa do banheiro público. Use *RESTROOM*.

Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Suggest three things to do with a friend tonight using 'Let's'. Use 'have' with at least one food or drink.
Let's have a pizza first. Then let's watch a movie together. After that, let's have a wine and relax a little bit.
2. Look around the room you are in right now. Describe three objects using possessive pronouns (my, your, his, her, our, their, its).
My phone is on the table. Our couch is really comfortable. My dog's ball is on the floor — its toy is always everywhere!
3. You arrive at a friend's house and it is very messy. What do you say to help? Use 'mess', 'Let's', and 'later' in your answer.
Wow, this place is a mess! Let's clean it up a little bit now. We can finish the rest later before your fiancee gets home.
