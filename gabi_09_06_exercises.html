Complete as Frases

📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. The doctor that the patient would need surgery after seeing the test results.
Use o passado simples de 'to foresee' — *PREVER*. O médico PREVIU algo no passado.
2. Health authorities warned the population about a possible disease spreading across the region.
A palavra significa *SURTO* — quando uma doença se espalha rapidamente. Em inglês: *OUTBREAK*.
3. Her symptoms were — just a slight headache and a runny nose — so she stayed home to rest.
Os sintomas eram *LEVES*, nada grave. A palavra em inglês é *MILD*.
4. The against the new virus was developed in record time by international researchers.
É a *VACINA* — substância injetável que protege contra doenças. Em inglês: *VACCINE*.
5. He couldn't train for two weeks because he had a bad and felt completely exhausted.
É um *RESFRIADO* comum — em inglês dizemos 'a cold'. Lembre: *COLD* como substantivo = resfriado.
6. After the final whistle, the opposing captain offered his to our best player as a sign of mutual respect.
É a *CAMISA DE TIME* — em inglês chamamos de *JERSEY*. Tradição famosa no futebol!

Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Have you ever had a cold with mild symptoms? What did you do to feel better?
Yes, last winter I had a mild cold. I had a slight sore throat and a runny nose, but no fever. I drank lots of tea with honey and lemon, rested at home, and took some medicine. After about three days, I felt much better. I didn't need to see a doctor because the symptoms were not serious at all.
2. Do you think scientists are able to foresee disease outbreaks before they happen? Why or why not?
I think scientists can sometimes foresee outbreaks by analyzing data, tracking infections, and studying patterns from previous diseases. However, it is very difficult to predict exactly when and where an outbreak will begin. Thanks to modern technology and international cooperation, we are definitely getting better at it.
3. Do you follow any sports team? Would you wear their jersey to show your support? Why?
Yes, I support a Brazilian football team. I would definitely wear their jersey because it makes me feel connected to the team and shows my passion for the sport. Wearing a jersey during a match creates a great sense of community with other fans. It is also a way of celebrating the players and the club's history.
