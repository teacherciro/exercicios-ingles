Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. Last Saturday, Ana to the beach and played in the sand.
Forma irregular do passado de 'to go'. A palavra-chave é *WENT*.
2. She pay for the tickets. Her friend paid.
Negação no Simple Past: *DIDN'T* + verbo na forma base.
3. Did you the report yesterday?
Após 'Did', use sempre o verbo na forma base. A palavra-chave é *WRITE*.
4. I love my sister. I need to call right now.
Pronome objetivo de 'she'. A palavra-chave é *HER*.
5. The mood at the World Cup incredible next year!
Use o auxiliar de futuro + BE. A estrutura é *WILL BE*.
6. Ana decided to every morning before breakfast.
Phrasal verb que significa 'malhar'. A expressão é *WORK OUT*.

2. Where did you go last weekend? Did you drive there or did you walk?
