Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Have you ever gotten a ride from someone? Who was it and where did you go?
Yes! Once I got a ride from my aunt. She drove me to the shopping mall because it was raining heavily outside.
2. If you were a police officer at the border, how would you catch a smuggler?
I would check all the luggage very carefully and use a scanner. I would also watch for people who look nervous or carry unusually heavy bags.
3. Why is it important to keep both hands on the driving wheel while driving?
It is important because you need to react quickly in emergencies. With both hands on the wheel, you have much better control of the car.
