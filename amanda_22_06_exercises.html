2
Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. The coach talked to the players after the game.
Após preposições, o verbo vai para o gerúndio (-ING). A preposição aqui é 'after'. Use *FINISH* no gerúndio.
2. Amanda is the student learns English every week.
Pronome relativo que identifica 'the student'. Em português: 'que'. Use *THAT* — aqui ele é obrigatório!
3. The was wet and slippery during the match.
Local onde o jogo de futebol acontece. Em português: *CAMPO*.
4. She me an invitation to the soccer game yesterday.
Passado irregular de 'send' (enviar). Use *SENT*. Lembre: SEND não usa preposição antes do objeto!
5. The made an incredible save in the final minute of the game.
O jogador que defende o gol. Em português: *GOLEIRO*.
6. I think Brazil's greatest strength is the attack.
Conjunção equivalente a 'que' em português, ligando duas orações. Use *THAT* — mas atenção: aqui ele é opcional!

Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Have you ever watched a soccer game where the teams tied? How did you feel about the result?
Yes, I have! Once I watched Brazil tie with Argentina 0-0. It was a tense game, but I was a little disappointed because I was hoping Brazil would win. The goalkeeper on Brazil's side was amazing though — he saved everything!
2. Think of a sport or activity you enjoy. Describe what happens before and after the event, using at least one preposition + ING structure.
I really enjoy watching volleyball. Before starting the game, the players warm up and practice their serves. After winning a point, they celebrate together on the field. The most important thing is to keep the pace and not let the other team score.
3. Imagine you are inviting a friend to watch a soccer game in Holland. What would you write in the invitation?
Hey! I sent you an invitation to watch Brazil play in Holland next Saturday. I really hope you can come! The field is beautiful and the atmosphere is incredible. It doesn't matter if the teams tie — it's going to be an amazing experience. Let me know before Friday!
