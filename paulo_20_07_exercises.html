1. Do you do any training at your job or school? What kind of training do you do?
Yes, I do training at my job. Every month, my company offers online training about new software. The training usually lasts about two hours and I learn a lot from it.
2. What is the currency of Brazil? Do you know the currency of any other countries?
The currency of Brazil is the Real. I also know that the currency of the United States is the dollar, the currency of Europe is the euro, and the currency of Japan is the yen.
3. Why is training important when you start a new job?
Training is very important when you start a new job because you need to learn the rules, the systems, and the tasks. Good training helps you work faster and make fewer mistakes.
