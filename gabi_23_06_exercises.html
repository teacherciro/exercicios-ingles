Perguntas Abertas

🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Have you ever used a heating pad? What was it for, and did it help?
Yes, I have! I used a heating pad last winter when my shoulders were really sore after sitting at the computer for too long. It helped a lot — the warmth relaxed my muscles and I felt much better after about twenty minutes.
2. Think of your favorite movie or TV show. Can you describe the plot in three or four sentences?
My favorite show is a mystery series. The plot follows a detective who investigates strange crimes in a small town. Each episode reveals a new clue, and just when you think you know what happened, there's a huge twist. The final season's plot was the most exciting of all.
3. What sector would you like to work in, and what does someone need to be savvy about to succeed in that area?
I would love to work in the healthcare sector. To be truly savvy in that field, I think you need strong communication skills, deep medical knowledge, and the ability to stay calm under pressure. Being tech-savvy is also becoming more important as hospitals adopt new digital systems.
