2
Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. The city government started a public to hire a construction company for the new bridge.
LICITAÇÃO pública em inglês = public *TENDER*
2. The music is too loud. Can you turn down, please?
Com phrasal verb separável, o PRONOME vai entre o verbo e a partícula. *IT* representa 'the music'.
3. She ought fix the potholes on this road. It is very dangerous.
*OUGHT TO* sempre precisa de 'to' antes do verbo base
4. The gravel road needs . There are too many potholes and it is getting dangerous.
MANUTENÇÃO em inglês = *MAINTENANCE*
5. Don't throw out. We can recycle that bottle.
Pronome *IT* no meio do phrasal verb THROW OUT
6. The landslide was a terrible disaster for the whole community.
Estrutura de ênfase: *SUCH* + a/an + adjetivo + substantivo

4
Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. What do you think the city government should do to fix the roads in your neighborhood? Use at least two modal expressions (should, must, have to, need to, ought to).
I think the government must fix the potholes and pave the gravel roads as soon as possible. They also need to do regular maintenance to avoid collapses. They ought to start a public tender to hire a good construction company.
2. Tell me about five things you have to do every day. Use 'must', 'have to', or 'need to' in your answers.
I must wake up early for work every day. I have to check my emails every morning. I need to exercise at least three times a week. I also have to cook dinner for my family and I must pick up my kids from school at four o'clock.
3. Describe a person or a place you really admire using 'such a'. What makes them so special?
My grandmother is such a kind person. She cooks such a delicious food and always makes everyone feel welcome. Her house is such a cozy and warm place. I love spending time with her — she is such a great storyteller too!
