Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. My flew to the United States last summer.
Quem são as pessoas que te criaram? Não confunda com 'país' (country)! - *PARENTS*
2. We need to go through before we can leave the airport.
É onde as autoridades verificam sua bagagem ao entrar em outro país - *CUSTOMS*
3. Brazil played Argentina in the final and scored three times.
Indica oposição — jogar 'contra' alguém - *AGAINST*
4. She put her passport and phone in her so she could access them easily on the plane.
A bagagem que você leva com você dentro da cabine do avião - *HAND LUGGAGE*
5. I rooted for my team this season, but they lost both games.
Aconteceu duas vezes - *TWICE*
6. There is a between the train station and the customs area.
Lugar onde você troca seu dinheiro por moeda estrangeira - *CURRENCY EXCHANGE*

4
Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Do you prefer to travel with hand luggage only, or do you always check luggage? Why?
I prefer to travel with hand luggage only because I don't want to wait at the airport. It is also faster to go through customs when I don't have checked luggage.
2. Did you ever root for a team that played against a big rival? What happened in the game?
Yes, I rooted for Brazil against Argentina last year. It was a great game. Brazil scored twice and won in the end. I was very happy!
3. Imagine you arrive at a foreign airport. What do you need to do between landing and leaving the airport?
First, I go through customs and show my passport. Then I pick up my checked luggage. After that, I look for a currency exchange to get local money. Finally, I find the train station or a taxi to go to the hotel.
