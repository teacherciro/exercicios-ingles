Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. I would love to new friends at the party tonight.
Use o verbo *MEET* (encontrar/conhecer) no infinitivo após 'to'
2. Would you going to the movies with us this weekend?
Use o verbo *ENJOY* (curtir/gostar) no infinitivo
3. She has important appointments this week and cannot come.
A palavra *SEVERAL* significa 'diversas/várias' — mais de duas, mas não muitas
4. I need to cancel my at the movie theater tomorrow morning.
A palavra *APPOINTMENT* significa 'compromisso' — algo agendado com hora marcada
5. I would prefer to stay home go out in the rain.
Use *THAN* para fazer comparações, com o sentido de 'do que'
6. The for the cinema tickets can be made online or at the door.
A palavra *PAYMENT* vem do verbo 'to pay' e significa 'pagamento'

Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Would you enjoy going to the movie theater with friends or would you prefer to watch movies at home? Why?
I would enjoy going to the movie theater with friends because the big screen and the sound are amazing. However, I would also enjoy staying home sometimes because it is more comfortable.
2. How many appointments do you usually have in a week? Would you like to have fewer?
I usually have at least two or three appointments during the week. Yes, I would like to have fewer because I would enjoy having more free time to relax.
3. Tell me about someone interesting you met recently. Where did you meet them?
I met a very friendly person at school last month. She enjoyed the same music as me and we enjoyed talking for a long time. I would love to meet her again soon.
