Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. We can see a river from the small farm.
Dica: O rio fica nas REDONDEZAS, bem pertinho da fazenda. Use *NEARBY*.
2. The weather says it will snow this winter.
Dica: Estamos falando da PREVISÃO do tempo. Use *FORECAST*.
3. John and sister rode horses on the farm all afternoon.
Dica: A irmã pertence a ele (John). Use o pronome possessivo *HIS*.
4. We watched a beautiful from the top of the hill.
Dica: Eles assistiram ao PÔR-DO-SOL do alto da colina. Use *SUNSET*.
5. She spent the summer at her cousin's small farm.
Dica: Ela passou TODO o verão na fazenda. Use a palavra *WHOLE*.
6. Waking up at 4 AM in winter just to see the sunrise?
Dica: Acordar às 4h não VALE A PENA. Use a expressão *IT'S NOT WORTH IT*.

Perguntas Abertas

🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. What is your favorite season of the year and why? Use at least three words from the lesson vocabulary.
My favorite season is fall. Actually, I love the cool weather and the beautiful sunsets nearby the mountains. The whole season feels magical — it's not worth it to stay indoors!
2. Describe a small farm using at least four vocabulary words from the lesson.
My cousin's small farm is nearby our city. The whole place is amazing — there are horses, a nearby river, and the sunsets are beautiful. The weather forecast says it will be warm this summer, so we are planning to visit!
3. Your friend wants to wake up at 4 AM in winter to take photos of the sunrise. What do you say? Use 'it's not worth it' and at least one possessive pronoun.
I think it's not worth it! The weather forecast says the temperature will be below 10 degrees. Trust me — your health is more important than a photo. Actually, we can watch a beautiful sunset in the afternoon instead. That's my plan!
