Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Do you cheer for a team in the World Cup? Which team and why?
Yes, I cheer for Brazil in the World Cup. I think they play beautiful soccer and I feel very proud when they win a match.
2. What can go wrong at a big event like the World Cup?
Many things can go wrong. For example, some people resell tickets at a very high price, the venue can get too crowded, and the weather can be bad with a lot of fog.
3. Is there an 'elephant in the room' when people talk about Brazilian soccer today? What is it?
Yes, I think the elephant in the room is the quality of the coach. People watch the games and curse at the TV, but nobody wants to say directly that the team needs big changes.
