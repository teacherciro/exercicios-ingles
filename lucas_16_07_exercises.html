Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. I need an extra because I'm feeling cold tonight.
Peça ao hotel quando estiver com frio. A palavra em inglês é *BLANKET* (cobertor).
2. She up in a small town before becoming a famous fashion designer.
Passado irregular do verbo 'to grow' (crescer) — *GREW*.
3. Please call if you'd like meals or drinks delivered to your room.
Serviço do hotel que traz comida ao seu quarto — *ROOM SERVICE*.
4. My is confirmed for three nights starting this Friday.
Reserva feita com antecedência — *BOOKING*.
5. She will launch the new webstore to the influencers .
Quando ela vai lançar a loja? Adicione uma expressão de tempo futuro — *NEXT WEEK* ou similar.
6. Could you please send extra to room 412? We just came back from the pool.
Item de tecido usado para se secar — *TOWELS* (plural).

Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Have you ever stayed at a hotel? What did you ask for at the front desk?
Yes, I have stayed at a hotel a few times. At the front desk, I asked for a room with a double bed and confirmed that breakfast was included. I also requested an extra pillow because I prefer sleeping with two.
2. If your hotel room was not comfortable, what would you ask for? Use as many hotel words as you can.
If my room was not comfortable, I would call the front desk and ask for extra blankets, an extra bed sheet, and more towels. If the bed was too small, I would request a room with a double bed instead of a single bed.
3. Imagine you are a sales manager launching a new hotel brand. What would it be called and what special services would it offer?
I would launch a hotel brand called 'CasaLux.' It would offer breakfast included in every booking, 24-hour room service, and extra pillows and blankets for all guests. The front desk would be open all day and night to help anyone who needed something.
