Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. What did you do last weekend? Use at least three past tense verbs from the lesson in your answer.
Last weekend I woke up late on Saturday. I ate breakfast with my family and then we walked to the park. In the evening, I listened to music and studied English. It was a relaxing day.
2. What is the difference between 'to listen to' and 'to hear'? Give one example of each from your own life.
The difference is that 'to listen to' is voluntary — you choose to pay attention to something. For example: I always listen to music when I run. 'To hear' is involuntary — it happens without you choosing. For example: I heard my neighbor's dog barking last night.
3. Describe an amazing place you visited or would like to visit. Where was it or where is it? What did you or would you do there?
I was in the northeast of Brazil last year. It was an amazing place! I walked on beautiful beaches, ate fresh seafood, and found some beautiful shells near the water. The place was wonderful and I would definitely recommend it to anyone.
