Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. It was so hot at the gym that I started to after just five minutes.
Quando o corpo está com calor ou nervoso, ele faz isso. *SWEAT*
2. Can you the kids from school today? I have a very long meeting.
Phrasal verb que significa ir buscar alguém em algum lugar. *PICK UP*
3. This dessert has sugar — it's not good for your health!
Use esta expressão com substantivos INCONTÁVEIS em contexto negativo/excessivo. *TOO MUCH*
4. She raised one and gave me a very suspicious look.
Parte do rosto localizada logo acima dos olhos. *EYEBROW*
5. The accountant said he would send the financial report
Expressão que significa 'o mais rápido possível'. Muito usada no trabalho. Abreviada como ASAP. *AS SOON AS POSSIBLE*
6. After reading the quarterly results, the manager looked very and called an emergency meeting.
Adjetivo que descreve como uma PESSOA se sente — preocupada. Sinônimo de 'worried'. *CONCERNED*

4
Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. When do you sweat the most — because of heat, exercise, or stress? How do you feel when it happens?
I sweat the most when I exercise at the gym. Sometimes I also sweat when I'm stressed before an important meeting or presentation. It's uncomfortable, but I know it's completely normal!
2. Think about your daily routine. Are there activities you are usually not in the mood for? What do you do to change your mindset?
On Monday mornings, I'm usually not in the mood to wake up early and start working. I try to drink coffee, listen to music, or make a to-do list to feel more motivated and organized.
3. Imagine your accountant calls to say there are too many expenses in your budget. What would you say or do? What benefits could you cut to balance things out?
I would feel very concerned! I would ask my accountant to send a detailed report as soon as possible. Then I would review all my expenses and try to cut some non-essential benefits to balance the budget.
