Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. My mom watches her favorite every night right after dinner.
Como se diz 'novela' em inglês? *SOAP OPERA*
2. She her daughter after her grandmother, which is a beautiful tradition.
Passado do verbo 'to name' (nomear, dar um nome). *NAMED*
3. I have a appointment this afternoon — my nails are a total mess!
Serviço de cuidado das unhas, igual em português! *MANICURE*
4. The photographer set up his so the camera wouldn't shake during the long exposure.
Suporte de três pernas para câmeras. *TRIPOD*
5. The doctor checked her levels and said they were too low, so she needs medication.
Glândula do pescoço. Pronúncia: THAI-roid. *THYROID*
6. Honestly, I rude people who talk loudly on the phone in public.
Como dizer 'não suporto'? Duas palavras! *CAN'T STAND*

Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Do you watch soap operas? Which one is your favorite and why?
Yes, I watch soap operas sometimes. My favorite right now is Renascer because the story is very dramatic and I love the characters. I can't stand the slow episodes, though — I always fast-forward!
2. Is there something you really can't stand doing in your daily routine? Tell me about it.
I can't stand waking up early on weekdays. I feel so tired and I can't focus until I have my coffee. I also can't stand waiting in long lines at the supermarket — it drives me crazy!
3. Have you ever entered a contest? What kind was it, and what happened?
Yes! I entered a cooking contest at work last year. I named my dish 'Grandma's Secret' because it was based on my grandmother's recipe. I didn't win, but everyone loved my food and it was a great experience.
