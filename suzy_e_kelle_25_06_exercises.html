Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Describe the weather in your city today. Is it cloudy, foggy, rainy, or bright? How does the weather affect your daily activities?
Today the weather is cloudy and a little rainy. I don't enjoy foggy days because I can't see well when I drive. On bright days, I feel much happier and more energetic, and I prefer to spend time outside.
2. Imagine you work as a trainer at a stud farm in France. Describe your daily routine. What do you do right away when you arrive at work?
If I worked as a trainer at a stud farm in France, I would arrive early every morning. Right away, I would check on the horses and make sure they were healthy and well-fed. Then I would install any new equipment in the basement and plan the training sessions for the day.
3. What is the difference between 'as' and 'like' in English? Explain in your own words and give one example sentence for each.
We use 'as' before a profession or role. For example: 'She works as a sculptor.' We use 'like' to give examples of similar things. For instance: 'I enjoy watching sports like horse racing and tennis.' Both mean 'como' in Portuguese, but they are used in very different situations.
