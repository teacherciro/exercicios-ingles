Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Is there a song, smell, or place that reminds you of someone special? Tell me about it.
Yes! The smell of sunscreen reminds me of my grandmother. We used to spend summers at the beach together when I was a kid, and she always put sunscreen on me before we went in the water. Every time I smell it now, I think of her.
2. Do you know anyone who is very down-to-earth? What do they do that makes you see them that way?
My dad is very down-to-earth. Even though he's successful at work, he never brags about it. He treats everyone the same — from his boss to the cleaning staff. I really admire that about him.
3. Has anything ever sent shivers down your spine? Describe the experience.
Yes! Last Halloween, I watched a scary movie alone at night, and there was a jump scare that sent shivers down my spine. I screamed so loud that my neighbor knocked on my door to check if I was okay!
