Complete as Frases
✏️
📝 Preencha os espaços e clique em "Verificar"!
1. I picked up at school this afternoon.
Com pronomes, o objeto vai ENTRE 'pick' e 'up'. Use *HIM* para 'ele/o'.
2. My mom always polenta on cold days — it's a family tradition.
Na cozinha, sempre use o verbo *MAKE* no lugar de 'do'.
3. Can you the car? I think the engine is frozen.
Para LIGAR motores ou máquinas, use o verbo *START*.
4. We went on a beautiful near the waterfall last Sunday.
A palavra significa *TRILHA* em inglês.
5. We where to travel for the holidays yet.
Use o Present Perfect negativo com *HAVEN'T* + particípio passado.
6. The doctor said there is a of iron in your blood — you should eat more liver.
A palavra significa *FALTA* de algo.

Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. Have you ever been on a hike? Where did you go, and how was the experience?
Yes, I have! I went on a hike in the mountains last year. It was beautiful but tiring. We walked for about three hours through the forest and stopped by a waterfall.
2. Have you ever been fooled or scammed? What happened, and what did you learn from it?
Yes, once I almost got scammed online. Someone tried to sell me a fake concert ticket. Luckily, a friend warned me in time. I learned to always check the seller's profile and reviews before buying anything.
3. What do you usually make for dinner? Do you enjoy cooking, or do you prefer eating out?
I usually make pasta or rice with chicken for dinner. Sometimes I make polenta, which is a family comfort food. I enjoy cooking, but only when I'm not in a hurry.
