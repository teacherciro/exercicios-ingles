Perguntas Abertas
💬
🖊️ Responda em inglês! Clique em "Ver modelo" para se inspirar.
1. What do you usually do on a cold and rainy day? Do you turn on a heater?
On a cold and rainy day, I usually stay home, turn on the heater, and cook pasta. I also like to watch movies and drink hot tea. It is my favorite kind of day!
2. Think of a time when you felt hopeful about something. What was it? Did it work out?
Once, I was very hopeful that my team would win an important game. I even made a bet with my friend. They won in the last minute, and I was so happy!
3. Is it important to be strong and healthy to win in sports? What else does a team need?
Yes, I think being strong and healthy is very important in sports, but a team also needs good communication and trust. If the players do not work together, even the strongest team can lose.
